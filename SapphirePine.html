SapphirePine

Project Type: Print

Tools: InDesign

Collaborators: SapphirePine Co-founders

SapphirePine is a startup I met during my time at the SkyDeck accelerator at UC Berkeley. They build furniture and milled wood products out of Sierra Nevada-native trees that have been killed by drought and bark beetle. Below are the business cards I mocked up for them. I kept the designs clean and minimalistic, keeping in line with SapphirePine's modern and sophisticated brand.
