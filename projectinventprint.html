Project Invent Print

Project Invent is a nonprofit organization that builds invention programs where students design technologies to positively impact people in their communities. I assisted Project Invent during its early stage, helping launch their brand through the creation of promotional materials. My creative pieces include business cards, digital/print pamphlets and booklets, and one-pagers.
