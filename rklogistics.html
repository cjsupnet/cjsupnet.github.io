RK Logistics

I assisted the Pilothouse design agency with creating a couple of homepage designs for RK Logistics. RK Logistics is a Fremont-based company that provides various supply chain services from manufacturing support to order fulfillment. In this design, I chose to place the most essential aspects of the company at the top, choosing different shades of color to make each pop out from the blue/white color scheme of the site.
