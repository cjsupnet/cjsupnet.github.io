Catherine Supnet

Media & Marketing Manager, SkyDeck

Hello folks!

I am a self-taught web and print designer in search of job opportunities in website design. My interest in design stemmed from my experience working at SkyDeck, a UC Berkeley-affiliated accelerator, where I worked for over 2 years and was exposed to the amazing innovations and projects brought on by UC Berkeley startups. At SkyDeck, I grew from creating promotional materials on Microsoft Word (see 1947 Partition Archive Print Projects) to building materials on Adobe Photoshop and InDesign (see SkyDeck Print Projects), to constructing wireframes and interactive prototypes of a website using Keynote, Sketch, and Adobe Experience Design (see SkyDeck Web Projects). I hope to be able to further my understanding of design and user experience in a job setting that can enable me to build on what I have learned. I am currently working towards being able to code what I design, taking online courses in HTML and CSS on both Codeacademy and Udacity during my spare time.
