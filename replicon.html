Replicon

Replicon is a software company with employees around the globe, including the United States, Canada, India, Australia, and the United Kingdom. They pioneered Time Intelligence, a new approach to time management that elevates time to improve businesses' operational productivity, performance, and profitability.

As a contract worker, and eventually as a full-time employee, I worked within the design team to uplift Replicon's visual design. Our design team focused on revamping webpages and creating web banners, infographics, ebooks, and print collateral (brochures, case studies, white papers, datasheets). In addition, our team worked alongside Marketing to visualize concepts for slide decks and alongside the Product to design illustrations for Replicon's new product features.
