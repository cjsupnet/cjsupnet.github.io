Replicon

Project Type: Web Design, Print, Illustration

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, XD, InDesign, WordPress

Collaborators: Marketing (includes Product Marketing, Copywriting, SEO, and Design), Sales, Product, Web, and CEO

Replicon is a time & attendance and project time tracking software company with employees around the globe, including the United States, Canada, India, Australia, and the United Kingdom.

I currently work within a 2-person Design team to revamp Replicon's visual design. Our work includes designing webpages and creating web banners, infographics, ebooks, slide decks, and print collateral (brochures, case studies, white papers, datasheets). We operate within the Marketing Team but we collaborate closely with the Web Team (based in Bangalore), oftentimes helping them update content on Replicon's WordPress-hosted website.
