Replicon

Replicon is a software company with employees around the globe, including the United States, Canada, India, Australia, and the United Kingdom. They pioneered Time Intelligence, a new approach to time management that elevates time to improve businesses' operational productivity, performance, and profitability.

As a contract worker, and eventually as a full-time employee, I worked within the design team to uplift the corporate Replicon brand. I assisted our senior designer with creating web banners, ebooks, infographics, white papers, and datasheets.
