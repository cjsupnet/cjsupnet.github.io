SkyPortal

The SkyPortal was a project initiated in the Summer of 2016 to provide the SkyDeck community of startups, Advisors, and Industry Partners with an internal site that facilitates access to contacts, upcoming events, and resources.

I served as the Project Manager, overseeing our amazing team of two software developers and a marketing intern. In addition, I served as the UI and UX designer, guided by a SkyDeck Advisor specializing in product design. Incorporating the principles of flat design into my work (to create a simple and usable user experience) , I built the initial wireframes using Keynote and eventually built an interactive prototype using Adobe Experience Design. In addition to PMâ€™ing and designing, I was responsible for adding content to our site database, using Mongo Express, under the guidance of our developers.

The site was launched in August 2016, but is still a work in progress. Our team continues to work hard on adding features and updating existing ones to provide our users with a more pleasant web experience.

Tools used: Keynote, Sketch, Adobe Experience Design, Mongo Express
