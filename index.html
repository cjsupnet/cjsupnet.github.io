About me

I am currently SkyDeck’s Design & Media manager, creating and managing its web, digital, and print materials. I also serve as an in-house design consultant, assisting SkyDeck’s startups with design related work (eg. digital illustrations, pitch decks, banners, handouts).

I am working on specializing in UI/UX design. So far, web and app design challenges make up the majority of my portfolio. Although they are not real-world projects, they enable me to practice building various site and app pages. If you’d like to connect, please reach out to me! I’d love to work alongside a real design team.
