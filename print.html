Demo Day Spring 2016 Booklet

I incorporated the minimalist principles of negative space and simple typography into my work and combined this with vibrant space imagery to create a booklet that was both bold and elegant in design.

1947 Partition Archive

Voices of Partition

As the 1947 Partition Archive’s Assistant Events Coordinator, I created and managed the promotional materials of their Voices of Partition Global Panel Discussion events. The only resource I had to create promotional materials was Microsoft Word. I hadn’t taught myself how to use Creative Cloud software during this time. For the poster on the top-left, I played around with layers and opacity, combining a total of 4 images. The two remaining posters were inspired by minimalist poster designs. I took photos of my own hand-drawings of silhouettes, uploaded them on Microsoft Word where I was able to extract the silhouettes and play with their color, and incorporated them into a new document, where I assembled the remaining content.

Tool used: Microsoft Word
