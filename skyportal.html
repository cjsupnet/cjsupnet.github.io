SkyPortal

The SkyPortal provides the SkyDeck community of startups, Advisors, and Partners with an internal site that facilitates access to contacts, upcoming events, and resources. I was the Portal's UI designer, guided by a SkyDeck Advisor specializing in product design. Incorporating the principles of flat design into my work, I built the initial wireframes on Keynote and eventually built an interactive prototype using Adobe XD. In addition to designing, I was responsible for maintaining the site's database, using Mongo Express, under the guidance of our developers.
