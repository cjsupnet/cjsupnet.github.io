Project Invent

Project Type: Print, Illustration, Animation, Branding

Timeframe: February 2018 - Present

Tools: Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects

Collaborators: Connie Liu (Project Invent Founder & Director)

Outcomes: 12 corporate sponsors secured, $100K+ raised, 777 teachers using curriculum, Presence in 23 countries

Project Invent is a nonprofit that provides a comprehensive curriculum to teach and support students in designing technologies that positively impact their communities. I am assisting Project Invent during its early stage, helping launch their brand through the creation of illustrations and marketing materials (eg. print & digital ads, animations, one-pagers, business cards, slide deck templates).
