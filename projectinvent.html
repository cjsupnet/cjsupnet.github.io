Project Invent

Project Type: Print, Illustration, Branding

Timeframe: February 2018 - Present

Tools: Illustrator, InDesign

Collaborators: Connie Liu (Project Invent Founder & Director)

Project Invent is a nonprofit organization that builds invention programs wherein students design technologies to positively impact people in their communities. I am assisting Project Invent during its early stage, helping launch their brand through the creation of illustrations and promotional materials (eg. print & digital ads, one-pagers, business cards, presentation decks).
